About Me

Read more to get to know me a little better.

Hello there, my name is Tyler! I'm a web developer living in Edmonton, Alberta. Highly reliable and motivated, I'm someone who puts my all into any project I work on, which is the level of dedication I'm looking forward to bringing to any future team.

I studied at the Northern Institute of Technology (NAIT) through their Digital Media and IT program with a concentration in web design and development, graduating in the Spring of 2023. Through school, I've gained experience with the different roles and pieces involved in building a website, including content writing, graphic design, UI/UX design, prototyping, and much more. What I have found in the last few years is that, above everything else, I really enjoy working on development. There is something satisfying about putting the pieces together and having everything working efficiently and looking nice.

I also like the challenges development brings. It can be difficult sometimes, but I enjoy the problem solving part, and that is something that keeps me motivated. Additionally, I am a highly organized person with a good eye for detail, which is what I found partly helped web development click for me.

I would love the opportunity to join a team and contribute to building quality websites. I enjoy development and tend to my projects with great care and detail, so if you're interested or have any questions, feel free to message me through the button below!
